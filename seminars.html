As a part of our advocacy, the RMRG conduct symposia that focus on a particular aspect of regional music scenes such as government, the recording industry, the live music industry, women in live music scenes and so forth. We seek to bring together industry, practitioners, academics and policymakers to discuss aspects of regional music in Australia. We explore the role of music in regional communities and economies, its relationship to national and international contexts, and ways to facilitate regional music development.
