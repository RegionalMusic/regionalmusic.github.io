RMRG Seminar 6. Regional Music Education

The next of our RMRG Seminar series on music education in regional areas will take place on 11 April 2024.

Moderator and RMRG member Dr Graham Sattler will discuss the state of music education in regional areas with Dr Wendy Brooks (Young Conservatorium), Dr Heleen de Plessis (University of Otago), and Dr Alana Blackburn (University of New England).

This will be an online event via zoom. Registrations are below.
